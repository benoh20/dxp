# Phone Banking Script
## [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] — [DISTRICT NAME]

**Target Demographic:** [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
**Key Message:** [KEY_MESSAGE]
**Estimated Call Time:** 2–4 minutes

---

## INTRODUCTION

"Hello, may I please speak with [VOTER NAME]?"

*[If not available:]*
"Thank you — I'll try back another time. Have a great day." *[Mark as CB — Call Back.]*

*[If reached:]*
"Hi [VOTER NAME], my name is [CALLER NAME] and I'm a volunteer calling on behalf of [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] here in [DISTRICT NAME]. I'm not trying to sell you anything — I just have a quick question or two. Do you have a moment?"

*[If no:]*
"No problem at all — thank you for your time. Have a great evening." *[Mark as NA.]*

---

## PIVOT

"[CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is running because [KEY MESSAGE — one sentence]. We know that issues like [TOP ISSUE 1] and [TOP ISSUE 2] matter a lot to people in [DISTRICT NAME].

On a scale of 1 to 5 — with 1 being strongly opposed and 5 being strongly supportive — how would you rate your support for [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME]?"

*[Record the voter's response in your dialer or call sheet.]*

*[STRONG SUPPORTER (4–5):]*
"That's great to hear. [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is committed to [BRIEF POLICY POINT FROM RESEARCH]. We're counting on voters like you on [ELECTION DATE]."

*[PERSUADABLE (3):]*
"I appreciate your openness. Can I share a little more about where [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] stands on [TOP ISSUE]? [TAILORED TALKING POINT FROM RESEARCH]. Does that help clarify things?"

*[OPPOSED (1–2):]*
"Thank you for sharing that. I'll pass along your concerns. Have a good evening." *[End call politely — do not argue.]*

---

## ASK

*[For supporters and persuadables only:]*

"[ELECTION DATE] is coming up on [DATE]. Do you have a plan to vote — will you be voting in person or by mail?"

*[If by mail:]* "Great — have you already requested your ballot? If not, the deadline is [DEADLINE DATE]."

*[If in person:]* "Your polling place is [LOCATION / refer to lookup link]. Polls open at [TIME] and close at [TIME]."

"Thank you so much for your time, [VOTER NAME]. Have a great [day/evening]."

---

**Field Notes:**
- Log all responses (support level, issue flags, vote plan) immediately after each call
- Do not leave voicemails unless instructed by your organizer
- Do not call before 9:00 AM or after 9:00 PM local time
- If a voter asks to be removed from the list, mark as OPT-OUT immediately

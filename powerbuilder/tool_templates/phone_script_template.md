# Phone Banking Script
## [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] — [DISTRICT NAME]

**Target Demographic:** [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
**Key Message:** [KEY_MESSAGE]
**Estimated Call Time:** 2–4 minutes

---

## INTRODUCTION

"Hello, may I please speak with [VOTER NAME]?"

*[If not available:]*
"Thank you — I'll try back another time. Have a great day." *[Mark as Not Home.]*

*[If reached:]*
"Hi [VOTER NAME], my name is [CALLER NAME] and I'm a volunteer calling on behalf of [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] here in [DISTRICT NAME]."

*[If hangs up or Voter says they're not interested:]*
"No problem at all — thank you for your time. Have a great evening." *[Mark as Refused.]*

---

## PIVOT

"[CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is running because [KEY MESSAGE — one sentence]. We know that issues like [TOP ISSUE 1] and [TOP ISSUE 2] matter a lot to people in [DISTRICT NAME]. (FILL IN WHY YOU SUPPORT THIS CANDIDATE OR ISSUE. PERSONAL STORIES MAKE THE MOST IMPACT)

Can we count on you to support [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME]?"

*[Record the voter's response in your dialer or Virtual Phone Bank (VPB).]*

*[If strong yes (1)]:* "Great! Thank you for your support for [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME]. We really appreciate your vote and we're counting on voters like you for your support on [ELECTION DATE]."
- Mark as "1. Strong Support"
- Move to Vote Plan and Volunteer Ask

*[If hesitant yes (2)]:* "Great, thank you for considering [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME]. [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is committed to [BRIEF POLICY POINT FROM RESEARCH]. We really appreciate your vote and we're counting on voters like you for your support on [ELECTION DATE]."
- Mark as "2. Lean Support"
- Move to PERSUASION SCRIPT

*[If unsure (3)]:* "I appreciate your openness. This is an incredibly important election, and it's important that you have all the information you need to make an informed decision."
- Mark as "3. Undecided"
- Move to PERSUASION SCRIPT

*[If hesitant no (4)]:* "No problem, thank you so much for your time! If you have any further questions you can check out more info at (INSERT CANDIDATE/ORGANIZATION WEBSITE). Please consider supporting [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME]!"
- Mark as "4. Lean Oppose"
- End Conversation

*[If strong no (5)]:* "No problem, thank you for your time!"
- Mark as "5. Strong Oppose"
- End Conversation

---

## PERSUASION SCRIPT

"A lot of folks in [DISTRICT NAME] are concerned about [TOP ISSUE 1] and [TOP ISSUE 2]. [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] believes [BRIEF POLICY STATEMENT TIED TO RESEARCH FINDINGS — do not invent, draw from research].

Do any of those issues matter to you?"

*[Listen actively. Note the voter's response. If they raise a different priority, pivot to it:]*

"That's really important. Here's where [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] stands on that: [TAILORED TALKING POINT FROM RESEARCH]."

**Common Objection — [LIKELY OBJECTION 1 FROM RESEARCH]:**
"I hear that a lot. What [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] would say is [REBUTTAL TIED TO RESEARCH]. Does that make sense?"

**Common Objection — [LIKELY OBJECTION 2 FROM RESEARCH]:**
"That's a fair concern. The difference here is [REBUTTAL TIED TO RESEARCH]."

---

## ASK

*[Record the voter's response in your dialer or Virtual Phone Bank (VPB).]*

"[ELECTION DATE] is coming up on [DATE]. Do you have a plan to vote — will you be voting early in person, by mail, or on Election Day?"

*[Record voter's response in MiniVAN]*

*[If early in person:]* "Great, Early Vote starts on [DATE] and ends [DATE]. Remember that your early vote location may not be the same as your Election Day polling place, so be sure to look up your polling place and hours at [VOTING WEBSITE]."

*[If by mail:]* "Great — have you already requested your ballot? If not, the deadline is [DEADLINE DATE]."

*[If in person:]* "Your polling place is [LOCATION / refer to lookup link]. Polls open at [TIME] and close at [TIME]."

*[If unsure:]* "No problem, making a plan to vote is important but can feel confusing. What time and days do you think you'll have time to vote?"

---

**Field Notes:**
- Log all responses (support level, issue flags, vote plan) during each call
- Do not leave voicemails unless instructed by your organizer
- Do not call before 9:00 AM or after 9:00 PM local time
- If a voter asks to be removed from the list, mark as REFUSED and OPT-OUT immediately

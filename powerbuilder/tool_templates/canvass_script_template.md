# Door-to-Door Canvassing Script
## [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] — [DISTRICT NAME]

**Target Demographic:** [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
**Key Message:** [KEY_MESSAGE]
**Estimated Conversation Time:** 3–5 minutes
**Leave Behind:** [LIT PIECE / PALM CARD]

---

## OPENING

*[Knock on door. When voter answers, smile and maintain eye contact.]*

"Hi, my name is [CANVASSER NAME], and I'm a volunteer with the [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] campaign here in [DISTRICT NAME].

Is this [VOTER NAME]?"

*[If yes — continue below.]*
*[If no — ask if they're home. If unavailable, leave lit and note the visit in MiniVAN.]*

---

## PIVOT

"Great! I'm out today because [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is running to [KEY MESSAGE — the change or goal this candidate/issue represents in one sentence].

A lot of folks in [DISTRICT NAME] are concerned about [TOP ISSUE 1] and [TOP ISSUE 2]. [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] believes [BRIEF POLICY STATEMENT TIED TO RESEARCH FINDINGS — do not invent, draw from research].

Do any of those issues matter to you?"

*[Listen actively. Note the voter's response. If they raise a different priority, pivot to it:]*

"That's really important. Here's where [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] stands on that: [TAILORED TALKING POINT FROM RESEARCH]."

**Common Objection — [LIKELY OBJECTION 1 FROM RESEARCH]:**
"I hear that a lot. What [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] would say is [REBUTTAL TIED TO RESEARCH]. Does that make sense?"

**Common Objection — [LIKELY OBJECTION 2 FROM RESEARCH]:**
"That's a fair concern. The difference here is [REBUTTAL TIED TO RESEARCH]."

---

## ASK

"We're asking supporters to [CALL TO ACTION — e.g., vote on [ELECTION DATE] / request a mail ballot / volunteer].

Can we count on your support?"

*[STRONG SUPPORTER (4–5):]*
"Wonderful! Here's a card with more information. [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] really appreciates it. Have a great [day/evening]."

*[UNDECIDED (3):]*
"I understand — this is an important decision. Here's some information to look over. Is it okay if someone follows up with you closer to [ELECTION DATE]?"

*[OPPOSED (1–2):]*
"I appreciate your time. Have a great [day/evening]."

---

**Field Notes:**
- Record support level and issue flags in MiniVAN/VAN before leaving the block
- Do not argue — thank opposed voters and move on
- If no answer after two knocks, leave lit and mark as NA

# Text Message (SMS) Templates
## [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] — [DISTRICT NAME]

**Platform:** Peer-to-Peer (P2P) Texting
**Target Demographic:** [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
**Note:** Each message must be under 160 characters. Use [NAME] as the personalization placeholder.
Always identify the sender and include an opt-out on first contact.

---

## INITIAL OUTREACH

> Hi [NAME]! I'm [CALLER], volunteering for [CANDIDATE] in [DISTRICT]. Can we count on your vote [ELECTION DATE]? Reply STOP to opt out.

**Character count:** ~130 — adjust [CANDIDATE] and [DISTRICT] to fit within 160 chars.

**If no response after 48 hours — send FOLLOW-UP A below.**

---

## FOLLOW-UP A — Issue Hook

> [NAME] — [CANDIDATE] is fighting for [TOP ISSUE] in [DISTRICT]. Election Day is [DATE]. Got 2 min to learn more? [LINK]

**Character count:** ~120

---

## FOLLOW-UP B — Urgency

> [NAME] — [ELECTION DATE] is almost here! [CANDIDATE] needs your vote. Polls open [TIME]. Any questions? Reply here.

**Character count:** ~110

---

## GET-OUT-THE-VOTE (GOTV) — Send Election Day Morning

> Today's the day, [NAME]! Polls close at [TIME]. Vote [CANDIDATE] — find your polling place: [LINK]

**Character count:** ~100

---

**Compliance Reminders:**
- Always identify who is sending the text (campaign name or candidate name)
- Include opt-out language ("Reply STOP") on every first contact
- Do not send texts before 9:00 AM or after 9:00 PM local time
- Use a licensed P2P texting platform — do not use autodialer/ATDS for voter contact
- Maintain opt-out list and suppress immediately on all future sends

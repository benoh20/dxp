# Digital Ad Copy Block
## [CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] — [DISTRICT NAME]

**Platforms:** Facebook / Instagram / Display
**Target Audience:** [TARGET_DEMOGRAPHIC]
**Key Message:** [KEY_MESSAGE]
**Flight Dates:** [START DATE] – [END DATE]

*A/B test at least two variations. Pause underperformers after 72 hours and scale the winner.*

---

## VARIATION A — Issue-Led

**Headline (under 40 chars):**
[TOP ISSUE] is on the ballot in [DISTRICT].

**Body Copy (under 125 chars):**
[CANDIDATE/ISSUE NAME] is fighting for [POLICY POINT tied to research]. Join us on [ELECTION DATE].

**CTA Button:** Learn More

---

## VARIATION B — Contrast / Stakes

**Headline (under 40 chars):**
The stakes are too high to sit this one out.

**Body Copy (under 125 chars):**
On [ELECTION DATE], [DISTRICT] voters decide [WHAT IS AT STAKE]. [CANDIDATE] has a plan.

**CTA Button:** Stand With [CANDIDATE]

---

## VARIATION C — Community Voice

**Headline (under 40 chars):**
[DISTRICT NAME] deserves better.

**Body Copy (under 125 chars):**
[CANDIDATE] has fought for [COMMUNITY] for [X] years. This [ELECTION DATE], vote for someone who gets it.

**CTA Button:** Learn More

---

## VARIATION D — Direct GOTV Ask

**Headline (under 40 chars):**
Will you vote on [ELECTION DATE]?

**Body Copy (under 125 chars):**
Early voting opens [DATE]. Make your plan to vote at [LINK].

**CTA Button:** Make My Voting Plan

---

**Creative Notes:**
- All copy must be grounded in research findings — do not invent statistics or quotes
- Use high-contrast visuals with candidate photo or district imagery
- Facebook/Instagram: image ratio 1:1 or 4:5 for feed; 9:16 for Stories
- Avoid text-heavy images (Facebook penalises >20% text on creative)
- Tag UTM parameters on all links for conversion tracking
